by Richard Stark

The brand Chrome Hearts was founded in 1988. The owners and designers of this line are Richard and Laurie Lynn Stark, who create their clothing and signature designs with the look and feel of a natural born biker. Today, Chrome Hearts (sometimes also known as CH) stores can be found largely throughout the US, Asia and Europe.

The brand currently offers jewelry, sunglasses, clothing, accessories, home goods, custom Rolex timepieces, and furniture. The brand takes pride in literally making everything you can imagine. They have created their own world, and it is apparent once you walk into each Chrome Hearts boutique.
